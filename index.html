Our Rack

We at Trestle Automation value not only your board, but your time as well. Ditch your lock, and simply tap your RFID-enabled student ID to lock and unlock a slot for your board. Our sustainable racks run off of our solar-powered, intelli-rack technology.

Secure

Exceptionally durable
deadbolt locking system.

Quick

Leave your lock at home,
forget your combination,
and secure your board in seconds.

Sustainable

Solar powered and constructed
using recycled materials.

Easy

Simply place your board into a slot, tap your ID card,
and enter your slot number to lock.
